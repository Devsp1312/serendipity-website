Privacy by default

Your voice stays yours.

Audio never leaves the device

Recording, transcription, and interest extraction all happen on-device overnight. Raw audio auto-deletes after two days.

Anonymous by design

No email, no name, no account. A random token is your identity — the system only ever knows user1, user2, user3.

Mute button, always in your hand

Press once to mute the mic. The e-ink display shows a muted icon so you (and anyone nearby) can see it's off.
